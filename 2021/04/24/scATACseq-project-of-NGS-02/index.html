Setting and Test

Prepend the Cell Ranger ATAC directory to your $PATH. This will allow you to invoke the cellranger-atac command.

To ensure that the cellranger-atac pipeline is installed correctly, use cellranger-atac testrun. This test can take up to 60 minutes on a sixteen-core workstation.
